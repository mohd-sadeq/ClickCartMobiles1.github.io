You know fresh meat tastes different?

At Zappfresh, we believe in tradition, values and the goodness of fresh meat. Experience the taste, texture and tenderness of farm fresh meat delivered straight from high quality farms. No more compromises, no inconvenience, just the unmistakable, sweet smell of juicy, all-natural meat.

Farm to table, same day delivery

To ensure consistent availability of fresh meat, we are redefining practices for the meat industry with high quality farms, hygienic slaughter houses and state-of-the-art cold chain supply.

We say no to chemicals

Fresh meat is delivered to you the same day the animal is butchered, through a unique end-to-end cold chain supply that ensures farm freshness and zero contamination. Our meat is tastier and healthier with no harmful chemicals, preservatives, artificial flavors and growth hormones.
